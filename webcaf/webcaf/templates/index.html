Submit a WebCAF self-assessment

The WebCAF self-assessment allows you to assess your organisation’s critical systems.

This is stage 3 of GovAssure, the cyber assurance process used to understand the level of cyber resilience across government.

WebCAF is based on the National Cyber Security Centre's Cyber Assurance Framework (CAF) (opens in new tab).

Before you start

You must have completed stage 1 and stage 2 of GovAssure. This means you will have:

  • completed your organisation’s scoping document
  • identified all your systems for assessment
  • chosen the the CAF profile of each system being assessed (baseline or enhanced)
  • nominated a GovAssure lead in your organisation, to complete the self-assessment
