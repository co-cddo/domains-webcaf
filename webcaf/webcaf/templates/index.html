Complete a WebCAF self-assessment

The WebCAF self-assessment allows you to assess your organisation’s critical systems.

This is stage 3 of GovAssure, the cyber assurance process used to understand the level of cyber resilience across government.

The current GovAssure WebCAF is based on version 3.2 of the National Cyber Security Centre's Cyber Assurance Framework (CAF) (opens in new tab).

Providing evidence

! Warning You do not need to add links to your evidence in this version of WebCAF.

You must create a separate repository to store evidence securely and share links to it. The assessor or peer reviewer will need access to this repository in Stage 4 in order to check your self-assessment responses.

There is more about creating an evidence repository at Stage 3 Provide your CAF self-assessment

Before you start

You must have completed stage 1 and stage 2 of GovAssure. This means you will have:

  • completed your organisation’s scoping document
  • identified all your systems for assessment
  • chosen the the CAF profile of each system being assessed (baseline or enhanced)
  • nominated a GovAssure lead in your organisation, to complete the self-assessment
  • created a repository for your evidence
