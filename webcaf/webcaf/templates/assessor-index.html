Review a WebCAF self-assessment

Use this service to review an organisation’s WebCAF self-assessment against National Cyber Security Centre's Cyber Assurance Framework (CAF) (opens in new tab).

You will be able to:

  • confirm the details of the system you are reviewing
  • view an organisation’s self-assessment
  • give your response to each IGP statement
  • provide comments where your response is different to the organisation’s selection for IGPs
  • write a summary for each contributing outcome
  • identify risks and make recommendations for contributing outcomes
  • comment on areas of good practice and suggest areas for improvement for each CAF objective
  • comment on the quality of the organisation’s self-assessment and supporting evidence
  • explain how and when you carried out your review

Session timeout

Your session will be ended if you are inactive for 90 minutes.
Any pages you saved earlier will be stored. We will delete your responses on pages you did not save.

Before you start

You must be an independent assurance reviewer contracted by an organisation to review their WebCAF self-assessment. The organisation must have given you user rights to access the self-assessment.

Before you begin your review, you may also find it helpful to have:

  • the organisation’s scoping document to refer to
  • access to the organisation’s evidence repository
  • the stage 4 guidance GovAssure (opens in new tab) open to read
