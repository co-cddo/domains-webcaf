Help

GovAssure uses the National Cyber Security Centre's Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF) (opens in new tab) to obtain an objective understanding of government cyber security as set out in the Government Cyber Security Strategy (opens in new tab).

For assessments in 2025-26, GovAssure is using CAF version 3.2 (opens in new tab). Please be aware that this is not the latest version published by NCSC.

Visit security.gov.uk (opens in new tab) for full GovAssure guidance.

WebCAF is the digital service that allows:

  • government organisations to provide a self-assessment against the CAF
  • third-party reviewers to review self-assessments and provide their own assessment

For information on how your data is used in GovAssure, visit the data usage page.
